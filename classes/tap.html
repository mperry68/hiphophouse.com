Tap Dance Classes

Rhythmic tap dance classes that develop musicality and coordination

About Tap Classes

Tap dance is a percussive dance form where dancers use their feet as instruments, creating rhythms and sounds. Our tap program emphasizes musicality, rhythm, and technique.

Students learn both traditional and contemporary tap styles, developing coordination, timing, and the ability to create complex rhythmic patterns with their feet.

Tap classes are excellent for developing musical awareness, coordination, and rhythm. We offer classes for all ages and skill levels.

What You'll Learn

Basic Steps

Master fundamental tap steps including shuffles, flaps, ball changes, and more.

Rhythm & Timing

Develop your sense of rhythm and learn to create complex rhythmic patterns.

Technique

Build proper tap technique including clarity of sound and proper weight distribution.

Combinations

Learn to combine steps into longer sequences and choreographed routines.

Class Levels

Beginner: Perfect for those new to tap. Learn basic steps, rhythm, and coordination in a fun, supportive environment.

Intermediate: Build on fundamentals with more complex steps and combinations. Focus on clarity and musicality.

Advanced: Challenging classes for experienced tappers. Work on intricate rhythms, speed, and performance quality.
