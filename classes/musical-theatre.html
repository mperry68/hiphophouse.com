About Musical Theatre Classes

Our musical theatre program combines the three essential elements of musical performance: acting, singing, and dancing. Students learn to integrate these skills to create compelling stage performances.

Classes focus on Broadway-style choreography, character development, and performance technique. Students work on numbers from classic and contemporary musicals, developing both technical skills and stage presence.

Musical theatre classes are perfect for students interested in performing arts, whether they're preparing for auditions or simply enjoying the art of musical performance.

Program Components

Dance Technique

Learn Broadway-style choreography and dance technique essential for musical theatre performance.

Acting & Character

Develop acting skills and learn to create believable characters through movement and expression.

Performance Skills

Build confidence and stage presence while learning to perform for an audience.

Musical Numbers

Work on complete musical numbers from popular shows, integrating all performance elements.

Who Should Take Musical Theatre?

Musical theatre classes are ideal for:

  • ✓ Students preparing for school or community theatre auditions
  • ✓ Dancers interested in expanding their performance skills
  • ✓ Anyone who loves musicals and wants to perform
  • ✓ Students looking to build confidence and stage presence
