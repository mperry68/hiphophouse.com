About Ballet Classes

Our ballet program provides classical training that builds strength, grace, and technique. Ballet is the foundation of many dance forms and develops discipline, posture, and artistry.

We offer ballet classes for all ages - from young children taking their first plié to adults discovering the beauty of classical dance. Our instructors follow traditional ballet methodology while creating a supportive and encouraging learning environment.

Students progress through levels based on their technical ability and experience, ensuring appropriate challenge and growth.

Program Levels

Pre-Ballet (Ages 3-5)

Introduction to ballet through creative movement, basic positions, and fun exercises designed for young children.

Children's Ballet

Structured ballet classes for children focusing on technique, coordination, and musicality in an age-appropriate setting.

Teen Ballet

Comprehensive ballet training for teenagers, building on fundamentals and introducing more advanced techniques.

Adult Ballet

Ballet classes designed for adults, whether you're a beginner or returning to dance. Focus on technique and enjoyment.
