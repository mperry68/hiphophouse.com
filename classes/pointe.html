About Pointe Classes

Pointe work is the pinnacle of classical ballet training, where dancers perform on the tips of their toes using specially designed pointe shoes. Our pointe program is designed for advanced ballet students who have developed the necessary strength and technique.

Students must have instructor approval before beginning pointe work, as proper preparation is essential for safety and success. Our instructors carefully assess each student's readiness and provide personalized guidance.

Pointe classes focus on building strength, proper alignment, and technique specific to dancing en pointe.

Pointe Requirements

Technical Foundation

Strong ballet technique and understanding of proper alignment are essential before beginning pointe work.

Strength & Conditioning

Sufficient ankle and foot strength, as well as core stability, are required for safe pointe work.

Age & Maturity

Students must be physically mature enough, typically starting around age 11-12, with instructor approval.

Regular Ballet Training

Ongoing ballet classes are required alongside pointe training to maintain and develop technique.
