About Hip Hop Classes

Our Hip Hop classes are the heart of HipHop House. These high-energy classes combine various street dance styles, including breaking, popping, locking, and freestyle movement. Students learn to express themselves through rhythm, style, and attitude.

Our experienced instructors teach both foundational techniques and current trends in hip hop dance, ensuring students develop a well-rounded understanding of this dynamic art form.

Classes are available for all ages and skill levels, from young children just starting their dance journey to adults looking to stay active and learn something new.

What You'll Learn

Foundation Moves

Master the fundamental hip hop moves and techniques that form the basis of all hip hop dance styles.

Musicality

Develop your sense of rhythm and learn to move in sync with the music, understanding beats and timing.

Style & Attitude

Learn to develop your own unique style and express your personality through movement.

Choreography

Work on learning and performing choreographed routines while also developing freestyle skills.

Class Levels

Beginner: Perfect for those new to hip hop dance. Learn basic moves, rhythm, and coordination in a supportive environment.

Intermediate: For dancers with some experience. Build on fundamentals and learn more complex combinations.

Advanced: Challenging classes for experienced dancers. Focus on intricate choreography and performance quality.
