Acro Dance Classes

Combining classical dance technique with acrobatic elements

About Acro Classes

Acro dance combines the fluidity and grace of dance with the strength and flexibility of acrobatics. This exciting style teaches students to seamlessly blend tumbling, contortion, and acrobatic tricks with dance movement.

Our acro program emphasizes safety, proper technique, and progressive skill development. Students build strength, flexibility, and coordination while learning exciting acrobatic elements.

Acro classes are available for various age groups and skill levels, with careful attention to each student's individual progress and safety.

What You'll Learn

Tumbling Skills

Learn cartwheels, handstands, walkovers, and more advanced tumbling passes.

Flexibility

Develop exceptional flexibility through targeted stretching and conditioning.

Strength Building

Build core and upper body strength essential for acrobatic movements.

Dance Integration

Learn to seamlessly combine acrobatic skills with dance choreography.

Safety First

Safety is our top priority in acro classes. Our certified instructors:

  • ✓ Provide proper spotting and assistance
  • ✓ Progress students at appropriate levels
  • ✓ Use proper equipment and mats
  • ✓ Focus on proper technique and form
