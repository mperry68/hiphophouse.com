Lyrical Dance Classes

About Lyrical Classes

Lyrical dance combines the technical elements of ballet and jazz with emotional expression and storytelling. Dancers learn to interpret music and lyrics through movement, creating beautiful, flowing performances.

Our lyrical program emphasizes both technical skill and emotional connection. Students develop grace, flexibility, and the ability to convey emotion through dance.

Lyrical classes are perfect for dancers who want to explore the expressive side of dance while building strong technical foundations.

Lyrical Dance Elements

Emotional Expression

Learn to connect with music and express emotions through movement and facial expression.

Fluid Movement

Develop flowing, graceful movements that connect steps seamlessly.

Technical Foundation

Build on ballet and jazz technique with turns, leaps, and extensions.

Musicality

Learn to interpret music and lyrics, matching movement to the emotional content of songs.

Who Should Take Lyrical?

Lyrical classes are ideal for dancers who:

  • ✓ Have some ballet or jazz experience
  • ✓ Want to explore expressive dance
  • ✓ Enjoy connecting movement to music
  • ✓ Are looking to develop performance quality
