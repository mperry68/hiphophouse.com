Jazz Dance Classes

Dynamic jazz classes focusing on technique, style, and performance

About Jazz Classes

Our jazz program combines the technical foundation of ballet with the energy and style of contemporary movement. Jazz dance is characterized by its sharp, dynamic movements, isolations, and expressive style.

Students learn a variety of jazz techniques including turns, leaps, and combinations set to popular and contemporary music. Our classes emphasize both technical precision and performance quality.

Jazz classes are available for all ages and levels, from young dancers exploring movement to advanced students refining their technique and style.

Jazz Dance Elements

Technique

Build strong technical foundation with proper alignment, turns, leaps, and jumps.

Style

Develop your personal style while learning various jazz dance styles and movements.

Performance

Learn to perform with confidence, expression, and stage presence.

Choreography

Work on learning and performing choreographed routines in various jazz styles.
